LoanGuard AI

Understand hidden risks in your loan agreements instantly

Upload one or more loan agreement PDFs
The system ingests dense legal documents, extracts risky clauses, explains them in plain English, estimates monetary impact, and compares multiple offers side by side.

1. Intelligent Document Ingestion

Automatically parses uploaded loan PDFs so borrowers do not need to manually read dense legal text.

2. Financial Risk Clause Detection

Finds prepayment penalties, foreclosure charges, hidden fees, auto-debit mandates, and late-payment clauses.

3. Plain Language Interpretation

Converts legal clauses into clear, borrower-friendly English explanations.

4. Clause-Level Risk Scoring

Each clause is rated Low, Medium, or High based on its restrictiveness and financial impact.

5. Monetary Impact Estimation

Quantifies the rupee impact of risky terms instead of only flagging them.

6. Overall Loan Risk Assessment

Generates a single risk score that reflects the agreement’s overall borrower risk profile.

7. Decision-Ready Summary Report

Produces a structured report that can be reviewed independently or shared with a financial advisor.

8. Loan Comparison Engine

Compares multiple agreements side by side by score, hidden cost, and penalty structure.
